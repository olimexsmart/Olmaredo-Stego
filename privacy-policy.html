Privacy policy for Olmaredo Steganography

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application “Olmaredo Steganography” for mobile devices that was created by Luca Olivieri. The Olmaredo Steganography is a steganography app, it implements a steganography algorithm that allows to hide messages into other media, in this case photos.  

 

What information does the Olmaredo Steganography obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information 

The Olmaredo Steganography obtains the information you provide when you give permission to the app to use certain data and functionalities of the device. This permission is not optional since the functionality is based on having access to these resources.

Olmaredo Steganography don't need to access the Internet and any kind of connection whatsoever, in fact it does not use any of them.

All data is processed offline on the device.

Automatically Collected Information 

Olmaredo Steganography DOES NOT collect data, the computation is a mere flux of data without permanent storage for exception of the final results. 

Does the Olmaredo Steganography collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Olmaredo Steganography does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. 

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Olmaredo Steganography?

Third parties don't have access to the data that Olmaredo Steganography elaborates, at least through the app itself.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop any permission of access to information by the Olmaredo Steganography easily by uninstalling the Olmaredo Steganography or through the setting of the device. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

Data is retained by Olmaredo Steganography for the time it is running. When the app is closed all data is lost.
